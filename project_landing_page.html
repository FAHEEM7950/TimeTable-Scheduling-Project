Core Features

🧠

Stress-Aware AI

Reads student stress votes and places the most stressful subjects in morning slots when students are most alert.

👨‍🏫

Smart Faculty Matching

Matches subjects to faculty by expertise, department, and experience — ensuring the best teacher for each subject.

⚡

Conflict Detection

Real-time validation prevents double-booking faculty across multiple sections simultaneously.

📊

Stress Analytics

Interactive dashboard showing hardest subjects, sleep patterns, and peak productivity trends per department.

🔄

Dynamic Substitution

When faculty are on leave, the AI finds the best available substitute from the same department automatically.

🌐

Multi-Role Portal

Five dedicated portals: Developer, College, Department Admin, Student, and Faculty — all connected.

How It Works

1
Student Stress Evaluation

Students log in and fill a comprehensive form covering sleep quality, stress levels, subject preferences, and peak productivity hours.

2
Faculty Preference Capture

Professors set their preferred teaching subjects for morning/afternoon slots and record their experience levels.

3
AI Algorithm Generates Timetable

The system sorts subjects by stress votes, matches them to the best-qualified faculty, and places stressful subjects in morning slots. Faculty preferences and conflict detection are automatically enforced.

4
Dynamic Substitution

When faculty are on leave, the AI automatically finds the best available substitute from the same department, prioritizing subject expertise and experience.
