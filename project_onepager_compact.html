AI-Powered Stress-Aware Timetable Generator

Where Student Wellness Meets Smart Scheduling

The Problem

Traditional timetables are static and ignore student well-being. Students are scheduled for stressful subjects at the worst times. 52% report poor scheduling impacts their mental health and performance.

Our Solution

A web-based intelligent scheduling system that collects student stress data and generates optimized timetables using a heuristic AI algorithm. Stressful subjects go to morning slots; faculty preferences are respected.

How It Works

1
Student Stress Evaluation

Comprehensive form: sleep, stress levels, subject preferences, peak productivity.

2
Faculty Preference Capture

Professors set preferred morning/afternoon slots and record experience.

3
AI Algorithm Generates Timetable

Sort by stress votes → match faculty → place in morning slots → conflict check.

4
Dynamic Substitution

AI finds the best substitute when faculty are on leave, by subject match.

Key Features

🧠 Stress-Aware AI

Prioritizes difficult subjects in morning slots.

👨‍🏫 Faculty Matching

Matches by expertise, department, experience.

⚡ Conflict Detection

Prevents double-booking faculty across sections.

📊 Stress Analytics

Dashboard: hardest subjects, sleep patterns.

🔄 Smart Substitution

Auto-replacement for absent faculty.

🌐 Multi-Role Portal

5 portals: Dev, College, Admin, Student, Faculty.
