AI-Powered Stress-Aware Timetable Generator

Where Student Wellness Meets Smart Scheduling

The Problem

Traditional college timetables are static and ignore the most important factor: student well-being. Students are often scheduled for their most stressful subjects at the worst possible times — early mornings, back-to-back periods, or when they're already burned out. 52% of students report that poor scheduling directly impacts their academic performance and mental health.

Our Solution

A web-based intelligent scheduling system that collects student stress and preference data, then generates optimized timetables using a heuristic AI algorithm. The system ensures stressful subjects are placed when students are most alert, while respecting faculty preferences and preventing scheduling conflicts.

How It Works

1
Student Stress Evaluation

Students log in and fill a comprehensive form covering sleep quality, stress levels, subject preferences, and peak productivity hours.

2
Faculty Preference Capture

Professors set their preferred teaching subjects for morning/afternoon slots and record their experience levels.

3
AI Algorithm Generates Timetable

The system sorts subjects by stress votes, matches them to the best-qualified faculty, and places stressful subjects in morning slots. Faculty preferences and conflict detection are automatically enforced.

4
Dynamic Substitution

When faculty are on leave, the AI automatically finds the best available substitute from the same department, prioritizing subject expertise and experience.

Key Features

🧠 Stress-Aware AI

Algorithm reads student stress votes and prioritizes placing difficult subjects in morning slots.

👨‍🏫 Faculty Matching

Subjects matched to faculty by expertise, department, and experience years.

⚡ Conflict Detection

Real-time validation prevents double-booking faculty across sections.

📊 Stress Analytics

Interactive dashboard showing hardest subjects, sleep patterns, and peak productivity trends.

🔄 Smart Substitution

Auto-replacement for absent faculty based on subject match and availability.

🌐 Multi-Role Portal

Separate dashboards for developers, colleges, admins, students, and faculty.
